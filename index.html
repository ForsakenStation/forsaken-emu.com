Everything You Need

📱

Play Anywhere

Runs natively on Android, iOS, Linux, Windows, macOS, and even in your browser. Your library follows you everywhere.

🎮

Every Console You Remember

From NES to PlayStation, Game Boy to Dreamcast — browse and launch games across 65+ retro gaming platforms with 159 emulator configurations.

✨

A Feast for the Eyes

Three beautiful view modes — Grid, List, and a cinematic 3D Carousel. Rich media display with box art, screenshots, videos, fan art, and more.

🕹️

Designed for Controllers

Full gamepad navigation with button indicators, plus comprehensive keyboard shortcuts for power users. Navigate your library without ever touching a mouse.

📁

Download, Play, Organize

Built-in download manager with queue and progress tracking. Automatic BIOS detection and download. Temporary and permanent storage with smart cleanup.

🌍

Free, Open, Global

MIT licensed, community-driven, and translated into 14 languages. Forsaken belongs to everyone.
